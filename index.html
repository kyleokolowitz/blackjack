Game Rules

Objective

Get a higher hand count then the dealer without going over 21.

Card Values

Face cards are all worth 10, Ace can be worth 1 or 11, and any others are worth their face value.

The Play

To start, players are dealt 2 cards and the dealer is delt 2 cards, one facing down. Players may choose to "hit" and be dealt another card or "stand" if they are satisfied with thier cards. Players can "hit" as many times as they like, but once they "stand", it's the dealers turn.

If either's hand goes over 21, they "bust" and the other scores. If both "stand", whoever has the hightest hand scores.
